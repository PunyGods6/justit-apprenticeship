About Me

Hey again! I'm Book Seenonmuang (Thai name Phanuwat), currently an Associate Engineer (C3) at Sainsbury's and working towards my Level 4 Software Development apprenticeship with Just IT and aspiring to become a C4 software engineer here at Sainsbury's.

I was born in Bangkok Thailand, and moved to London at 7 years old. I am passionate about solving real-world problems through software and working as part of a collaborative, forward-thinking team.

My main focus right now is sharpening my skills in JavaScript, React, Next.js and eventually move onto back-end technologies like GoLang, Node.js and AWS, while learning as much as I can about modern software practices in a commercial setting.

How to Work with Me

If you want to get the best out of working with me, here are a few things I appreciate:

  • Be clear and direct. Context is everything!
  • I love constructive feedback, especially if it helps me improve or learn something new.
  • Respect deadlines, but if anything changes, I would like to know early on.
  • I'm always open to collaborating and sharing ideas. Don't be afraid to pair up with me, I love bouncing ideas off others.
  • Keeping things positive and honest; it helps the team and makes work more enjoyable.
